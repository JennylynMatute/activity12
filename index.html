BACHELOR IN APPLIED COMPUTING TECHNOLOGY

The BACT is a four year degree program that focuses on the practical Aspect of the science of computing.The program develops core skill in multimedia technology, web mobile development and network systems administrations. Furthermore, it prepares learners with supervisory and management skills. The curriculum is derived from the BS Industrial Technology major in Computer Technology with CHED Certificate of Program Compliance(COPC) No. 31 series of 2021

Program Objectives:

The program prepare learners with the
practical skills in multumedia technology,
web mobile development and
network systems administration and
supervisorial skills to manage an organization
in this areas of competencies, engage in a
startup business or pursue a teaching career.

Specific Objectives:

The learner should be able to:

1. Demonstrate understanding of the importance of being a a ethical computing
professional when making work-related decisions.
2. Develops product of processes or applying their knowledge in computer or network
systems, and development of productivity tools.
3. Participate as an active member of a development service team with leadership roles
when appropriate.
4. Apply communicaton skills effectively to promote ideas,goals,processes of products.
5. Make positive contributions to the community with the skills and abilities learned in
the program.
6. Pursue lifelong learning for personal and professional growth through varied
experiental and fielbased oppurtunities.
